Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a manga written and illustrated by Gege Akutami and is published in Weekly Shonen Jump. The first chapter was published on March 5, 2018 in issue 14 of Weekly Shonen Jump.

Jujutsu Kaisen is one of my newest favorite animes firstly because of the art style and how the animation is done but also because it offers a dark and intense style that combines supernatural elements with demons and devil powers. I like the concept of cursed spirits, sorcery battles, and the struggle to protect humanity against malevolent forces. Jujutsu Kaisen is a must-watch for those who enjoy a darker tone, supernatural abilities, and a captivating narrative.
